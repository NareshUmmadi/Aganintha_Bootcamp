Follow Traffic Rules

Following traffic rules is important for the safety of all road users, including drivers, pedestrians, and cyclists. By following traffic rules, we can help to reduce the risk of accidents and injuries.

Obey all traffic signs and signals.

Drive at a safe speed.

Do not drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

Yield to pedestrians and cyclists.

Be aware of your surroundings and be prepared to stop for unexpected hazards.

Following traffic rules is not only important for safety, but it is also the law. By following traffic rules, we can help to create a safer and more orderly road environment for everyone.
