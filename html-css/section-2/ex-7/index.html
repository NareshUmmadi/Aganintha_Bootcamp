India Wins World Cup Match Against Pakistan

October 14, 2023

India defeated Pakistan in a thrilling World Cup match on October 14, 2023. The match was held in India, and Pakistan batted first. They were all out for 192 runs. India chased down the target successfully, losing only three wickets.

India's batsmen started their chase cautiously, but they soon opened up and scored runs at a good pace. India's captain, Rohit Sharma, led the way with a brilliant half-century. India eventually reached the target in the 47th over, winning the match by 7 wickets.

India's victory over Pakistan was a comprehensive one. They outbowled and outbatted their opponents.
